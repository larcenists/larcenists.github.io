Support

Direct support is available to Larceny users through the larceny-users mailing list. Users are encouraged to submit bug reports either through the Larceny bug database or by sending email to larceny@ccs.neu.edu.

Reference

Larceny supports several standards, including the following standards available online:

  • Revised5 Report on the Algorithmic Language Scheme (R5RS)
  • Extended R5RS Scheme (ERR5RS)
  • Revised6 Report on the Algorithmic Language Scheme (R6RS)
